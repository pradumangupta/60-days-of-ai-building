# Project

## 🚀 AI Pitch Tank – Investor Readiness Analyzer

Today, I built and deployed an AI-powered startup pitch evaluation platform that simulates how investors, venture capitalists, and startup advisors analyze startup ideas.

The platform enables founders to:

- Validate startup ideas
- Evaluate fundability
- Identify business risks
- Improve investor readiness
- Receive actionable startup feedback

# Problem Statement

Many founders spend months building products and preparing pitch decks without understanding how investors evaluate startups.

Common challenges include:

- Unclear value proposition
- Weak market validation
- Poor business models
- Lack of investor readiness
- No structured feedback

# Analysis Framework

The AI evaluates startups through:

### Problem Validation

Does the startup solve a painful problem?

### Market Analysis

Is the market sufficiently large and growing?

### Business Model Assessment

Can the startup become financially sustainable?

### Competitive Analysis

Does the startup have defensible differentiation?

### Founder Readiness

Is the startup ready for investor conversations?

A strong business model matters more than feature quantity.

## 2. Distribution Is Critical
