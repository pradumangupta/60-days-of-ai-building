# Project

## 🧩 Face Puzzle Challenge

Today, I built and deployed a browser-based Face Puzzle Challenge game designed to provide an engaging and interactive experience while demonstrating frontend development and UI/UX skills.

The application allows users to solve face-based puzzles by rearranging shuffled image pieces into their correct positions.

## Step 1

Load and split the face image into multiple puzzle pieces.

## Step 2

## Step 4

Validate puzzle completion.

## Step 5

Display success feedback once the puzzle is solved.
