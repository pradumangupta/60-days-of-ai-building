# Day 6 – AI Resume Optimizer

## Objective

Learn how AI can be used to optimize resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and improve recruiter readability.

---

## Overview

Today, I explored AI-powered resume optimization by comparing an existing resume with an ATS-friendly version generated using AI.

The goal was to understand how AI can improve resume structure, keyword alignment, readability, and overall effectiveness for AI Engineer roles.

---

```text
You are an ATS optimization expert and resume writer.

Rewrite my resume for maximum ATS parsing and recruiter readability, keeping every claim truthful.
```

## Key Learnings

### 1. ATS Systems Prefer Simple Formatting

Complex designs, tables, graphics, and multiple columns can negatively affect ATS parsing.

### 2. Keywords Matter

Relevant technical keywords increase visibility during resume screening.

### 3. Recruiters Scan Quickly

Clear structure and concise bullet points improve readability.

### 4. AI Can Accelerate Resume Improvement

AI helps identify keyword gaps, formatting issues, and content improvements in minutes.

---

## Biggest Insight

A resume should be optimized for both:

1. ATS Systems
2. Human Recruiters

Balancing both significantly increases the chances of getting shortlisted.
