# Project

## 🤖 AI Job Search Assistant

Today I built a personalized AI Job Search Assistant using Claude.

The goal was to simulate how AI can act as a career coach, recruiter, and job search strategist by analyzing:

- Professional Profile
- Job Search Preferences
- Career Goals
- Market Demand
- Skill Gaps

The assistant then provides personalized recommendations and career insights.
