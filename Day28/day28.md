# Project

## 🏥 AI Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator

Today, I built and deployed an AI-powered healthcare application designed to help patients and their families assess their readiness before hospital admission.

The simulator analyzes a patient's medical information, documentation, insurance status, and financial preparedness to generate a comprehensive admission readiness assessment.

# Problem Statement

Hospital admissions can be stressful and time-sensitive.

Patients often face challenges such as:

- Missing medical documents
- Incomplete insurance information
- Lack of previous medical records
- Financial uncertainty
- Poor understanding of admission procedures

These issues frequently lead to:

- Admission delays
- Increased patient anxiety
- Administrative bottlenecks
- Longer waiting times

This project aims to streamline the admission process through AI-powered readiness assessment.

# Key Features

## 📋 Patient Readiness Assessment

Evaluate:

- Medical Preparedness
- Documentation Completeness
- Insurance Readiness
- Financial Preparedness

---

## 📊 Admission Readiness Score

Generate an overall readiness score (0–100) indicating how prepared a patient is for hospital admission.

---

## 🩺 Medical Information Analysis

Analyze:

- Existing Medical Conditions
- Current Medications
- Allergies
- Previous Surgeries
- Recent Symptoms

---

## 📄 Documentation Checklist

Verify availability of:

- Government ID
- Insurance Card
- Referral Letter
- Previous Medical Records
- Lab Reports
- Prescription Records

---

## 💰 Financial Readiness Assessment

Assess:

- Insurance Coverage
- Cashless Facility Availability
- Budget Preparedness

---

## 🚨 Risk Assessment

Classify patient readiness into:

🟢 Fully Ready

🟡 Mostly Ready

🟠 Partially Ready

🔴 Not Ready

---

## 💡 Personalized Recommendations

Provide actionable guidance including:

- Missing document alerts
- Medical preparation suggestions
- Insurance recommendations
- Financial planning advice
