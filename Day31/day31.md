# 📡 Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## 📌 Project Overview

AI Supply Chain Control Tower is an intelligent monitoring and decision-support platform that provides end-to-end visibility across supply chain operations. It helps organizations monitor critical KPIs, detect disruptions, assess operational risks, and generate AI-powered recommendations for faster and more informed decision-making.

A modern supply chain control tower goes beyond dashboards by combining real-time visibility, predictive insights, and AI-assisted decision support to improve resilience and operational efficiency.

## ✨ Features

* 📊 Real-time supply chain monitoring
* 🚨 Intelligent alerts for operational disruptions
* 📦 Inventory and order visibility
* 🚚 Shipment tracking and logistics insights
* 🤖 AI-powered risk analysis and recommendations
* 📈 KPI dashboard for operational performance
* 🔍 Exception management and proactive issue detection
* 🌐 Interactive web-based interface
