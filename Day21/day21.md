# Day 21 – Build & Deploy an AI Carbon Footprint Calculator

## Objective

Build and deploy an AI-powered Carbon Footprint Calculator that helps users estimate their environmental impact based on daily lifestyle choices and provides actionable sustainability recommendations.

---

# Project

## 🌍 AI Carbon Footprint Calculator

Today, I built and deployed a web application that calculates an individual's estimated carbon footprint based on transportation, energy usage, food habits, and lifestyle patterns.

The application enables users to better understand how their daily activities impact the environment and encourages sustainable decision-making.

---

# Live Demo

🌐 https://foot-print-omega.vercel.app/

---

# Problem Statement

Climate change and sustainability are becoming increasingly important.

However, many individuals are unaware of:

- Their personal carbon emissions
- Major emission sources
- Lifestyle areas contributing most to environmental impact
- Actions that can reduce their footprint

This project aims to bridge that gap through an easy-to-use AI-powered application.
