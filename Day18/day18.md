# Day 18 – AI Brain Dump Organizer

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Brain Dump Organizer that transforms messy, unstructured thoughts into actionable and organized information.

---

# Project

## 🧠 AI Brain Dump Organizer

Today I built an application that helps users convert scattered thoughts, meeting notes, voice transcripts, and project ideas into structured outputs.

Instead of manually organizing information, users can simply paste their raw notes and let AI extract meaningful insights.

---

# Live Demo

🌐 https://brain-dumb.vercel.app/

---

# Problem Statement

Many people capture ideas in an unstructured format:

- Meeting Notes
- Voice Memo Transcripts
- Brain Dumps
- Project Planning Notes
- Random Thoughts

The challenge is converting this information into something actionable.

This application solves that problem using AI-powered analysis and organization.
