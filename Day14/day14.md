## Objective

# Project

## 🚩 AI Job Red Flag Detector

Today I explored how AI can help candidates analyze job postings beyond the job title and salary.

The goal was to identify:

- Unrealistic requirements
- Toxic workplace indicators
- Misleading remote work claims
- Hiring risks
- Company-related concerns

This creates a smarter and more strategic job search process.

---

# Problem Statement

Many candidates spend significant time applying for jobs without evaluating whether the opportunity is actually a good fit.

Common issues include:

- Unrealistic expectations
- Excessive responsibilities
- Poor work-life balance
- Hidden relocation requirements
- Unclear compensation structures
- Questionable company stability

This project demonstrates how AI can detect these risks before investing time in the interview process.

---

# AI Analysis Framework

The AI evaluates five major categories.

---

## 1. Unrealistic Requirements

Checks for:

- Excessive years of experience
- Long lists of required technologies
- Multiple roles combined into one position
- Contradictory expectations

Examples:

- Entry-level role requiring 5+ years experience
- Junior role requesting senior-level responsibilities
- One person expected to perform multiple specialized jobs

---

## 2. Toxic Workplace Signals

Identifies phrases commonly associated with unhealthy work environments.

Examples:

- Rockstar Developer
- Ninja Engineer
- Hustle Culture
- Wear Many Hats
- Fast-Paced Environment
- High Ownership Without Support

Potential Risks:

- Burnout
- Long working hours
- Lack of role clarity
- Poor work-life balance

---

## 3. Remote Job Authenticity

Detects hidden requirements behind remote job claims.

Checks for:

- Mandatory office attendance
- Relocation expectations
- Geographic restrictions
- Hybrid requirements disguised as remote

---

## 4. Hiring Risks

Evaluates:

- Missing salary ranges
- Vague responsibilities
- Excessive qualification demands
- Unclear hiring process
- Unrealistic expectations

---

## 5. Company Risks

Reviews:

- Layoff concerns
- Stability indicators
- Growth sustainability
- Employee feedback trends
- Organizational transparency

# Smart Interview Questions

### 1

Can you describe a typical work week for this role?

---

### 2

How is work-life balance measured and maintained?

---

### 3

What are the expectations during the first 90 days?

---

### 4

How does the company support employee growth and development?

---

### 5

Can you clarify remote work expectations and office requirements?
